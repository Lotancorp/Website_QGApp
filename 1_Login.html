Tutorial 1: Getting Started

Steps to Login

  1. Open the "Quartz Gallery Application".
  2. Click the Login button at the top of the screen.
  3. Enter your username and password in the provided form.
  4. Press Enter or click the Login button to proceed.
  5. If the login is successful, you will see a success message and can start using the application.
  6. To log out, click the Logout button at the top of the screen.

Important Notes

  • Ensure you use valid credentials to log in.
  • If you forget your password, contact the administrator for assistance.
  • Log out after completing your session to maintain account security.
